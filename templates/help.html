How to find your UL cookie

To connect your account we need the .AspNetCore.Identity.Application cookie from the Lebanese University website.

  1. Go to the UL login page and log in normally.
  2. Open developer tools (right-click → Inspect, or press F12).
  3. Go to the Application tab (Storage in Firefox).
  4. In the sidebar expand Cookies and select http://www.ulfg.ul.edu.lb.
  5. Find the cookie named .AspNetCore.Identity.Application.
  6. Copy its entire Value.
  7. Paste it into the cookie form.
Prefer not to do this every time? Sign in with your UL username and password instead — the monitor will then renew the cookie on its own when it expires.
