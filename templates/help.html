How to find your UL Cookie

To connect your account, we need the .AspNetCore.Identity.Application cookie from the Lebanese University website.

  1. Go to the UL login page and log in normally.
  2. Open Developer Tools in your browser (Right-click anywhere -> Inspect or press F12).
  3. Go to the Application tab (or Storage in Firefox).
  4. In the left sidebar, expand the Cookies section and click on http://www.ulfg.ul.edu.lb.
  5. Find the cookie named .AspNetCore.Identity.Application.
  6. Copy the entire Value of this cookie.
  7. Paste it into the form on our cookie update page.
